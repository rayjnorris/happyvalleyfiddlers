Directions from Lenoir:

Travel north on U.S. 321 from Lenoir. Turn right on N.C. 268 east. Travel 1 mile and turn right again to stay on N.C. 268. Follow N.C. 268 another 8.2 miles to Jones Farm. The gate entrance is on the right.

Directions from U.S. 421 in Wilkesboro: 

Take N.C. 268 west toward Patterson. The festival site is 18.4 miles from U.S. 421. The gate entrance is on the left.
