Preregistrations are open now!

Based on history and interest, individual categories will have a limited number of slots, but bands are unlimited, however we strongly recommend that bands pre-register. Registration will be on a first-come, first-served basis until the category is filled. Regardless of the number of entries, registration will end 15 minutes before the stage time for each competition. See chart below for maximum competitors per category. The festival management reserves the right to adjust maximum numbers if needed.

Avoid standing in line by pre-registering . Enter your information in the on-line form, or print out the appropriate form below and mail to: Tony Deal, 2950 Willowbrook Rd. Lenoir, NC 28645
